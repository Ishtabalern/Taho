Soya Beancurd

The most famous dessert of every Filipinos



What is this Dessert?

It's called Soya Beancurd! (Taho)

It is the most awaited food of the filipinos every morning. You can hear the vendor from far away shouting Taho and expect everyone from your neighborhood to go outside and buy some taho. It doesn't matter how old you are, you can't resist the taste of this sweet taho.


Also Available in

Strawberry Flavor

The most delicious flavor of taho is if it's combined with the most favourite fruit of all, Strawberry! This type of taho can only be found in the summer capital of the Philippines, Baguio City. It's because in Baguio city, there is a strawberry farm that you can visit. So they made this type of taho using the fresh strawberries that they harvested in the farm!


How much is Taho

It's Very Cheap!

It depends on size but the smallest size you can buy costs 5 pesos. The most common size everyone buys is the 10 pesos because it is the exact size for your cravings. Some shop sells taho more expensive than the one that selling in your village. They add more toppings than the original one but adding more can also give more taste in the Taho!
